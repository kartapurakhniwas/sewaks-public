OUR MISSION

Our Mission to Inspire, Empower, and Create Lasting Positive Change

Langar Sewa

Our charitable service provides daily free, wholesome food to hospitals and trains, spreading comfort and sustenance to those who need it most.

Rehabilitation Center

Addressing age-related or injury-induced decline, the board initiates a Rehabilitation center for underprivileged individuals, focusing on adaptation, education, and independence. Construction underway, facing funding challenges.

Music Classes

Elevating communities through the universal language of music. Our charitable trust proudly offers free music classes, fostering creativity, skill development, and joy for individuals of all ages and backgrounds.

Computer Education

Empowering futures through technology. Our charitable trust is delighted to offer free upcoming computer education classes, providing essential skills and opening doors to opportunities for all enthusiastic learners.
